Booking Policy

Clear booking expectations before the chair.

Simple expectations for booking, changing, or arriving for an appointment at AMIN.

Confirmations

Online bookings are confirmed once AMIN accepts the requested service, barber, date and time. Customers should provide accurate contact details so the team can send confirmations or follow up if a time needs to change.

Cancellations

If a customer needs to move or cancel an appointment, they should contact AMIN as early as possible. Longer services, colour services and after-hours bookings may require extra notice.

Late Arrivals

Late arrivals may shorten the appointment or require rescheduling if the delay affects the next client. AMIN will do its best to help, but barber availability cannot be guaranteed after the booked start time.

Pricing

Prices shown online are a guide based on the selected service. Final pricing may change if the service changes in-chair, extra time is required, or add-ons are requested.
