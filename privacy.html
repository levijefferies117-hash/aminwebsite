Privacy

Customer details are only used to manage enquiries and appointments.

Clear privacy wording for appointment requests, enquiries and customer communication.

Information Collected

When a customer books or contacts AMIN through the website, AMIN may collect their name, phone number, email address, selected service, preferred barber, appointment time and any notes they choose to provide.

How It Is Used

Customer information is used to manage appointment requests, contact customers about bookings, answer enquiries and provide grooming services. It is not sold to third parties.

Booking Tools

If AMIN chooses a custom booking system, appointment information may be sent to AMIN's email or calendar tools. If AMIN chooses to keep Square, bookings will be handled under Square's own privacy and booking terms.

Analytics

If analytics are added, they should be used to understand page visits and booking interest without selling customer information.
